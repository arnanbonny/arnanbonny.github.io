Superstore EDA — Finding $99,000 in Losses

An end-to-end exploratory data analysis on retail sales data. I deliberately corrupted a clean dataset with seven realistic data quality issues, cleaned it back with documented judgment calls, then investigated why nearly 1 in 5 orders was losing money. The analysis traced the losses to discounting policy and identified category-specific discount thresholds and structural loss-makers.

Tools: Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn
